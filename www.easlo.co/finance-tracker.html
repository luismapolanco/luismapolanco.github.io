Frequently asked questions

Who is this for?
This template is for individuals to businesses looking to track their finances and take back control of their spending.
Why Notion for finance tracking?
Notion is available on every platform, with customizable layouts and a wide range of components and views including board view, gallery view, table view and more.

Stacking up with advanced filter, sort, and search functions, Notion provides a powerful database to track years of financial data without paying any recurring fees.
Can I import my existing data into Notion?
Yes. You can import your Google Sheets and Excel (.xslx) files into Notion by exporting them as CSV files. Thereafter, you can use the "Merge with CSV" button in the ••• menu at the top right of the database to import your CSV into an existing database.
Can I sync the Notion data with my bank accounts?
