Frequently asked questions.

What is a Notion template?
A Notion template is any publicly shared page in Notion that can be duplicated. They allow you to duplicate other workflows and systems that you want to use.
How to duplicate a template?
After your purchase, you will receive a template link. Open the link, then click on duplicate on the top right corner, then choose the workspace you'd like to duplicate into.

If you're logged out or don't have a Notion account, you'll be prompted to sign in or create one first.
